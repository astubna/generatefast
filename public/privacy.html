Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

GenerateFast collects the following types of information:

  • Analytics Data: Pages viewed, time on site, clicks, language preference (via Google Analytics 4)
  • Domain Searches: The domain searches you make (to improve our AI generation)
  • Affiliate Clicks: When you click "Buy domain" links (to track conversions and pay affiliates)
  • Voluntary Information: Any feedback you provide

2. Cookies We Use

We use cookies for two purposes:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for the website to function (no consent needed)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics - tracks site usage to help us improve (requires your consent)

You can manage your cookie preferences using the cookie banner on our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Improving our AI domain generation algorithm
  • Understanding user behavior and preferences
  • Tracking affiliate commissions (to pay registrars for referrals)
  • Enhancing site performance and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics 4: For site analytics (Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Groq API: For AI domain generation (Privacy: https://groq.com/)
  • RapidAPI Domains API: For domain availability checking
  • Affiliate Programs: Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc. (they receive your domain interest data)

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Analytics data is stored by Google (encrypted, subject to their security)
  • Search queries may be logged for service improvement
  • No sensitive personal information (credit cards, passwords) is collected or stored
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

6. Data Retention

  • Analytics: Google Analytics retains data for 14 months
  • Cookies: Kept as long as needed (you can delete anytime)
  • Search History: Not permanently stored (used for immediate improvement only)

7. Your Rights (GDPR/GDPR-like)

You have the right to:

  • Access what data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Withdraw cookie consent anytime

To exercise these rights, contact: contact@generatefast.com

8. International Users

If you're in the EU, we comply with GDPR. If you're elsewhere, we follow international privacy standards. Cookie consent is required for analytics tracking in all regions.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We'll notify you of major changes via email (if you've provided contact info) or by updating this page with the new date.
